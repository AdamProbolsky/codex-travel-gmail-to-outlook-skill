# Formatting Examples

These examples are fictional. Do not replace them with real traveler data in a public repository.

## Hotel event

Subject:

```text
Hyatt House Sample Airport
```

All-day dates:

```text
July 31, 2026 through August 7, 2026
```

Show as:

```text
Free
```

Location:

```text
Hyatt House Sample Airport, 100 Example Drive, Sample City, ON A1A 1A1, +1 555-010-2026
```

Notes:

```text
Confirmation #: H0T3L12345
Guest: Example Traveler
Check-in: Friday, July 31, 2026 at 3:00 PM
Check-out: Friday, August 7, 2026 at 11:00 AM
Room type: 1 Bedroom King Suite with full kitchen
Cancellation policy: Cancel by 11:59 PM hotel time one day before arrival.
```
